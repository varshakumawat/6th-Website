Welcome to Medicenter

We meet the highest medical standards
ensuring a high level of safety

Pregnancy Clinic is a unique place that was created with women in mind. Our mission is to provide patients and their children with professional service, individual care, safety and comfort. We are distinguished by these features:

  • Many years of experience
  • 24/7 care for mother and baby
  • Qualified team of professors
  • Modern apparatus
  • Operating and delivery rooms
  • Neonatal intensive care room

Why Choose Us
